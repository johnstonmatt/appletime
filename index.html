Appletime. Appletime.

While with the spurs, Kawhi was known for his love of red apples.

One time after practice everyone decided to go out for a team dinner.

When it was his turn to order, Kawhi waived off the waiter and instead pulled out a bag of 12 apples.

Coach Popovich asked what he was doing and Kawhi simply replied, "Apple time, apple time."

He then ate all 12 apples with a knife and fork.
